Bio

Hello, my name is Benjamin Bergstrom and I am an editor at the entertainment marketing agency, Giaronomo Productions.

I’ve worked on all kinds of campaigns, home entertainment, theatrical, digital and for a multitude of different media types. Ranging from thrilling theatrical dramas to slow burn documentaries.

While working at Giaronomo I’ve had the pleasure of working with clients like Disney, HBO, Apple TV+, and many more! Including direct communication and feedback with said clients.

I hold a Bachelor of Science (Cinema and Photography concentration) from Ithaca College where I learned the basics of my skills.

Beyond that I love working on personal projects like writing, miniature model making, and Dungeons and Dragons.
